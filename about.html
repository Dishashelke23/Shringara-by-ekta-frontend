Our Story

Ishara by Ekta began as a vision — to celebrate handcrafted Indian textiles while empowering local artisans in Pune. Every kurti, every thread carries a story of resilience, creativity, and hope. Our collections are sustainably made and ethically sourced, ensuring beauty never comes at the cost of humanity or nature.
